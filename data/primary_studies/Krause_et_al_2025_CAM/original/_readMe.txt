Dataset: The influence of seagrass traits and environmental controls on soil organic carbon stocks in Chouy Pros Bay, Cambodia

Authors: Johannes R. Krause (jkrause@fiu.edu) and Chantha Chroeng and Matt Glue and Lim Mesa and Tharamony Ngoun and Chandara Tak and Daniel T.I. Bayley (Daniel.Bayley@fauna-flora.org) and James W. Fourqurean

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.25573/serc.28507154

The data release contains biomass and sediment profiles from seagrass meadows. The data itself is housed in separate .csv files which can by joined by relational attributes (ex. site_id).

Krause_et_al_2025_sites.csv - Contains site-level information.
Krause_et_al_2025_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores.
Krause_et_al_2025_biomass_cores.csv - Contains information on the above and belowground plant mass and community composition.

metadata.xml - Contains a full suite of descriptive metadata with all attribute and variables defined, units clarified, and geographic, temporal, and taxonomic coverages described according to Ecological Metadata Language Standards.

metadata.html - Is a simplified, visual and interactive version of metadata.xml for display purposes.
